Profiling your talks!

The next slide will be, by default, about "Sintax Highlight".
But, if you go to the toolbar(that one, in the top left of your screen), click on "settings"and then change the profile to "longer", the next slide becomes a different one.

Let's say you have two different kinds of audiences, and some slides are meant for one, or another...then you can just set the profile and go ahead!
